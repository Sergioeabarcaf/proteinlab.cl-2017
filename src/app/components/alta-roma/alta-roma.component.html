ProteinLab Presente en AltaRoma 2019, Roma, Italia.

AltaRoma

Is a hub of fashion shows and the presentations of couture houses, small ateliers, artisanal and tailoring establishments. It focuses on the promotion of ‘savoir faire’ intended as a means of fostering artisanal expertise and all forms of Made in Italian and international excellence. The Altaroma calendar features the names of numerous historic couturiers and new international haute couture houses who choose Rome to present their collections. Likewise, it aims to give exposure to the singular skills and strategies that characterize local industries renowned for their savoir faire in those sectors, arts and trades that have made Italy and Rome famous throughout the world.
