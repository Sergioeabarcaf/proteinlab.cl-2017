ProteinLab presents M2M collection at AltaRoma 2019

AltaRoma

AltaRoma is Rome’s celebrated fashion week, whose two annual appointments in January and July create innumerable opportunities for the meeting between historic Italian fashion houses and new international creative and production realities. Esta temproada de invierno 2019 for the first time at Altaroma, @mondo_digitale will animate a digital fabrication space devoted to biomaterial and wearable technology. ProteinLab fue uno de los invitados a participar con su colección M2M or imitation of nature realizada en conjunto con claudio paredes. M2M es la primera colección cápsula en Chile hecha enteramente con técnicas de fabricación digital

Collection M2M

“M2M or Imitation of Nature - Capsule collection” is a collaborative project in Design, Fashion and Technology developed between ProteinLab UTEM and costume designer Claudio Paredes.

The inspiration of M2M comes directly from the observation of the mathematical beauty of nature, which is manifested in the use of fractal designs and patterns found in it. The objective of this project is to explore the potential of the union of fashion and technology through the use of traditional haute couture design and development techniques, in combination with digital manufacturing technologies and interactive electronics. Technologies such as laser cutting, allow the application of designs, textures and dynamic and complex patterns directly on the fabric, as 3D printing facilitates the creation of biomimetic elements which are integrated into the garments.
