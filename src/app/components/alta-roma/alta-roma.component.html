ProteinLab presents M2M collection at AltaRoma 2019

From the 24th to the 27th of January 2019, in the city of Rome, Italy, the event will be held "Week of digital fashion" in the context of the Week of High Fashion in Rome. In this Context, the Design of Costumes and Digital Technologies project called "M2M o Imitation of nature ", carried out jointly by ProteinLab UTEM and the designer of Claudio Paredes costume has been selected to present its 12 looks that include 3D printing, laser cutting and electronics.

This project was selected in the contest "Digital Made", of the foundation of the same name in conjunction with AltaRoma (www.altaroma.it), both are Italian organizations focused on design and technology.

AltaRoma

Collection M2M

"M2M or Imitation of Nature", is a collaborative project in Design, Fashion and Technology that was developed for nine months between ProteinLab UTEM and the designer of Claudio Paredes wardrobe. The collection integrates the use of manufacturing tools digital such as 3D printing, laser cutting, and embedded electronics that provides ProteinLab in harmony with the traditional techniques of clothing and costume design contributed by Claudio Paredes.
